## CSV Import Checklist

Before you import into Notion, confirm these fields are clean:

1. Company names and contact emails are deduplicated.
2. Deal stages, lead sources, and owner names use consistent labels.
3. Dates are normalized before CSV import.
4. Project, task, meeting, invoice, and content rows have clear IDs or names for relation checks.
5. Row counts are checked after each Notion database import.
6. CRM template views are tested before the business OS becomes the live workspace.
