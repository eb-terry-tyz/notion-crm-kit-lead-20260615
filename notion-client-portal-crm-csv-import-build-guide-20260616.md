## How To Import And Build

1. Audit your current spreadsheet columns for contacts, companies, active deals, current clients, projects, and open tasks.
2. Clean duplicate names, inconsistent status values, missing next steps, and stale close dates before importing.
3. Import the CSV files into Notion databases and match each column to the intended property type.
4. Rebuild the CRM and client portal views from the guide: active pipeline, current clients, project delivery, meetings, follow-ups, and review dashboards.
5. Test the workspace with one real client workflow before moving the rest of your business process into it.

This CSV import workflow takes more care than duplicating a template, but it gives you a clearer system because your actual sales and delivery data shape the final workspace.
