## CSV Import Checklist

Before importing CSV files into Notion, standardize the columns that matter most for a CRM:

- Contacts: name, email, company, role, owner, lifecycle stage, and last contacted date.
- Companies: company name, industry, website, account status, and relationship owner.
- Deals: deal name, linked company, stage, value, close date, next step, and source.
- Projects: client, status, deliverable, deadline, priority, and internal owner.
- Tasks and meetings: due date, related client, related deal, notes, and follow-up action.

This avoids the common Notion import problem where every spreadsheet becomes an isolated table with inconsistent field names. A useful CRM import should preserve the operating rhythm of the business, not just copy rows into a workspace.
